Basic Feline and Canine Classifier

Using artificial intelligence and convolutional neural networks for the binary classification of dogs and cats.

The Project

This project is my first attempt at implementing machine learning and artificial intelligence. Using Keras and TensorFlow, I created a model that could train on labelled data and correctly identify cats and dogs with an accuracy of more than 80%.
